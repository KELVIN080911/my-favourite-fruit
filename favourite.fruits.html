My favourite food

Apples

An apple is a round, edible fruit produced by an apple tree Apple trees are cultivated worldwide and are the most widely grown species in the genus Malus.


Oranges

The orange, also called sweet orange to distinguish it from the bitter orange , is the fruit of a tree in the family Rutaceae.


Bananas

A banana is an elongated, edible fruit produced by several kinds of large herbaceous flowering plants in the genus Musa.
